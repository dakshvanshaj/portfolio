## How I Work with You
I don't claim to know your industry better than you do. Here's my honest process:

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>1. I Listen</h3>
  <p>You explain the problem. I ask about constraints, KPIs, and what you've already tried.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>2. I Research</h3>
  <p>I study your domain, explore 2-3 solution approaches (ML, heuristics, or simple automation), and assess feasibility.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>3. I Prove It First</h3>
  <p>Before writing production code, you get a feasibility report with rough ROI estimates. No surprises later.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>4. I Build & Deliver</h3>
  <p>End-to-end MLOps pipeline—automated, monitored, documented. You own it, not me.</p>
</div>

!!! info "Industry Agnostic"
    This process means I can tackle retail, logistics, SaaS, or manufacturing—because the process transfers, even when the domain changes.
