## What Happens on the Call?

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>I Listen</h3>
  <p>You walk me through your business problem, constraints, and what success looks like.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>I Dig Deeper</h3>
  <p>I'll ask about your data, KPIs, and what you've already tried (so we don't repeat failed experiments).</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>I Research</h3>
  <p>After we talk, I spend time researching 2-3 potential solutions tailored to your situation.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>You Get a Free Mini-Report</h3>
  <p>Within 48 hours, I send you a short feasibility memo with rough ROI estimates, technical approach, and honest advice—even if ML isn't the answer.</p>
</div>

<div class="process-step">
  <h3>You Decide</h3>
  <p>No pressure. If it makes sense to work together, great. If not, you still walk away with clarity.</p>
</div>
